## ğŸŒ About Us
Welcome to the Economic Brain Lab at Swinburne University of Technology, Melbourne.

We investigate the intersection of neuroscience, psychology, and economics to understand how the brain makes decisions about risk, reward, and value. Our research combines **neuroimaging** and **neurostimulation**, **behavioral experiments**, and **computational modeling** to uncover the mechanisms behind decision-making and metacognition.
